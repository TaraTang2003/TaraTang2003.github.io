1.1 线性模型(the linear model)

原理

回归方程 (regression equation), the expected value of yyy given xxx：

E(Yi∣xi)=β0+β1xiE(Y_i|x_i)=\beta_0+\beta_1x_i E(Yi​∣xi​)=β0​+β1​xi​

估计的回归方程 (estimated regression equation), the estimated value of E(Yi∣xi)E(Y_i|x_i)E(Yi​∣xi​):

yi^=β0^+β1^xi\hat{y_i}=\hat{\beta_0}+\hat{\beta_1}x_i yi​^​=β0​^​+β1​^​xi​

回归模型 (regression model)

the exact value of yyy:

y=E(Y∣x)+ϵ=β0+β1x+ϵy=E(Y|x)+\epsilon=\beta_0+\beta_1x+\epsilon y=E(Y∣x)+ϵ=β0​+β1​x+ϵ

the estimated value of yyy:

yi=yi^+ri=(β0^+β1^xi)+riy_i=\hat{y_i}+r_i=(\hat{\beta_0}+\hat{\beta_1}x_i)+r_i yi​=yi​^​+ri​=(β0​^​+β1​^​xi​)+ri​

ϵ\epsilonϵ (error) : 误差项的随机变量，反映除xxx 变化引起的 yyy 的线性变化之外随机因素对 yyy 的影响，满足 ϵ∼iidN(0,σ2)\epsilon \sim^{iid} N(0,\sigma^2)ϵ∼iidN(0,σ2)

  • iii : independent —— 样本相互独立 (every observation to be independent of every other observation)
  • iii : identically distributed —— 方差齐性 (the equality of variance assumption, (EOV))
  • ddd : distribute_d normally —— 误差项正态分布 (ϵi\epsilon_iϵi​ must be from a normal distribution, at least approximately)

配对t检验(the paired t-test)

配对 ttt 检验用于分析配对定量数据之间的差异对比关系。前提条件为配对数据的差值满足正态分布。配对数据是指相互连接或关联 (不相互独立)，样本量相同的数据。

The paired t-test is just an application of the one-sample t-test applied to differences.

二分类模型 (2-level categorical variable model)

哑变量 (Dummy Variable)

在构建回归模型时，

  • 如果自变量 xxx为连续性变量，回归系数 β1\beta_1β1​ (slope) 可以解释为：在其他自变量不变的条件下，xxx 每改变一个单位，所引起的因变量yyy的平均变化量；
  • 如果自变量xxx为二分类变量，例如是否饮酒（1=是，0=否），则回归系数 β1\beta_1β1​ (slope)可以解释为：其他自变量不变的条件下，x=1x=1x=1（饮酒者）与 x=0x=0x=0（不饮酒者）相比，所引起的因变量 yyy 的平均变化量。

当自变量 xxx 为多分类变量时，引入哑变量（Dummy Variable），又称为虚拟变量将各个多分类变量均转化为0-1变量。

以 Exam 和 Attend 为例

The 2-level categorical variable model：

Exam=β0+β1⋅AttendExam=β0,Attend=No(0)Exam=β0+β1,Attend=Yes(1)Exam = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \cdot Attend \\ Exam = \beta_0, Attend = No(0) \\ Exam = \beta_0 + \beta_1, Attend = Yes(1) Exam=β0​+β1​⋅AttendExam=β0​,Attend=No(0)Exam=β0​+β1​,Attend=Yes(1)

  • β0\beta_0β0​ is the mean exam mark for non-attenders
  • β0+β1\beta_0 + \beta_1β0​+β1​ is the mean mark for attenders

so β1 is the difference in mean mark for attenders compared to non-attenders.

To test the null hypothesis of no attendance effect, H0:β1=0H_0:\beta_1=0H0​:β1​=0.

p=1.27×10−6p=1.27 \times 10^{-6}p=1.27×10−6 , We have extremely strong evidence that attendance is related to exam score.

Regular attenders will obtain an increased exam mark of between 9.5 to 21.6 compared to non-attenders.

The expected exam mark of a student who regularly attends class is 9.5 to 21.6 marks higher than that of a non-attendee.

1.7 模型检验(checks of model assumptions)

EOV check

检测线性模型是否合适。

绘制 resuidual values - fitted values 散点图

由于 The residuals will be randomly scattered around 0，r-f scatter中红线期望为$ y=0$ 的水平直线。

如果红线呈曲线，则需要考虑广义线性模型中的变式。

normality check

检测样本是否满足正态分布。

直方图

根据直方图及去实现可以判断样本是否满足正态分布以及是否 左偏 (lef skewed)、右偏 (right skewed) 等。

qq图

Q-Q图是一种散点图，横坐标为某一样本的分位数，纵坐标为另一样本的分位数。通过Q-Q图可以判断样本与模型是否大致符合以及是否存在离群点。

outlier check

检测离群点。

Cook距离越大，此点对模型的影响越大，异常的可能性越大。an observation is deemed to be influential if:

• Removal of the observation changes any estimated parameter value by more than one standard error, or

• Its Cook’s distance is greater than 0.4
