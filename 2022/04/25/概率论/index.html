离散型分布

分布类型 表示 释义 fx(X) E(X) Var(X)
二项分布 With a probability p of “success” each Bernoulli trial, in n independent Bernoulli trials. X is the number of “successes”.
超几何分布 With M “successes” in a fifinite number of items N, there are X “successes” in n draws.
泊松分布 With a constant average rate of λ there are X of events occurring in a fifixed time independently and differently.
二项分布的极限分布（n极大，p极小）
负二项分布 With a probability p of “success” each Bernoulli trial, before the kth “success” there are X “failures”.
几何分布 With a probability p of “success” each Bernoulli trial, in n independent Bernoulli trials.before the first “success” there are X “failures”.
