IELTS题解

本文最后更新于：2022年11月14日 上午

C8 T1

listening

Section 1

  1. Loads of people have phoned you.

    loads of = lots of

  2. a brochure from a summer festival-mainly Spanish music.

    brochure /ˈbrəʊʃə®/ n 资料（或广告）手册

    • a travel brochure 旅游手册

    pamphlet /ˈpæmflət/ n 传单

  3. They had a video with all the highlights of the festival at a stand in the lobby to the library, so I heard them.

    highlight n 最精彩的部分

    • One of the highlights of the trip was seeing the Taj Mahal.
    • The highlights of the match will be shown later this evening.

    lobby n 公共建筑物进口处的）前厅，大厅

    • a hotel lobby 旅馆大厅

  4. spoil ourselves

    To do what you want to do

    • I am on a diet, but I am really hungry. I will spoil myself with a large pizza!
    • I’m going to spoil myself tonight with a good movie and a few beers.

  5. postcode

    邮政编码

  6. ·booking form

    订单

    • Studio photography 摄影工作室
      • Aila studio photography 爱罗拉摄影工作室
    • deposit n 订金
    • refundable n 可退换的
    • balance n 尾款
    • prior n 先前的
      • be paid prior 先付清
      • Please give us prior notice if you need an evening meal. 需用晚餐者，请预先通知我们。
    • photo session 照相时间
    • all parties agree to the fees above n (契约) 当事人，一方
    • hereby accept the amount charged adv 在此

  7. refreshment

（在公共活动场所供应或销售的）饮料，小食

  • Light refreshments will be served during the break. 中间休息时有点心供应。

  1. pianist/ bargain

  2. good heavens 天哪

  3. Wish we were students-look!

Children, Students and **Senior Citizens** get a 50% discount on everything.

If only! 要是是就好了

Section 2

  1. brief you about what the tour will be about

    brief v 向（某人）介绍情况

    The officer briefed her on what to expect. 长官简要向她说了一下可能遇到的情况。

  2. As far as the amount of time you'll need goes, if you bring a school group you should plan on allowing a minimum of 90 minutes for the visit.

    As far as 至于/就…来说

    As far as I can see, you’ve done nothing wrong. 依我看，你没有做错任何事。

    As far as I can tell , she’s enjoying the course. 据我判断，她喜欢这门课程。

  3. If you're going to have lunch at the museum you will, of course, have to allow more time.

    allow : to let sb have sth v 给予

    You’re allowed an hour to complete the test. 你们有一个小时的时间来完成这次测验。

    have to allow more time 需要更多时间

  4. Then outside the museum at the back

    outside of the … at the back 在…后面

  5. foyer / lobby / vestiblue / entrance area

  6. When the students come into the museum foyer we ask them to check in their backpacks with their books, lunch boxes, etc, at the cloakroom before they enter the museum proper.

    the museum proper = main part of the museum

    Let’s stop here and drop off our bags before we enter the hotel proper.

  7. handout / worksheet

    （发给学生的）课堂讲义 handout = material distributed to the individuals in a group. It may be a worksheet, or it may be just reading material.

    （学生做的）活页练习题 worksheet = a sheet with a written exercise on it, to be done either in class or as homework. A worksheet may be a handout or may be included in a workbook. In the US we even have worksheets we can use to compute our income taxes, so the term can be applied in non-academic settings.

  8. survive threat 度过生存威胁

  9. 散词

    cafe 咖啡馆，小餐馆（供应饮料和便餐，在英美国家通常不供应酒类）

    backpack 背包

    cloackroom 衣帽间，寄存室 （coat room, luggage room, entrance area, vestibule, foyer, or other small room before the main area of a building. The people are checking their belongings to make sure they are ready to go before they enter the main area of the building.）

    exhibit /ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/ n 展品

    documentary n 纪录片

    etc ˌ*/et ˈsetərə/*

reading

Passage 1

  1. details of the simultaneous efforts of different societies to calculate time using uniform hours

    simultaneous adj 同时发生（或进行）的；同步的

    simultaneous translation/interpreting 同声传译

    simultaneous equations 方程组

    The game will be broadcast simultaneously on TV and radio. 比赛将同时在电视和广播中进行转播。

    interpret v 口译

  2. resemble

    resemble v 像，与……相似

    So many hotels resemble each other. 许多酒店看上去都差不多。

    assemble v 聚集，集合

    All the students were asked to assemble in the main hall. 全体学生获通知到大礼堂集合。

    assemble evidence/data 收集证据╱数据

    assembled company 全体讲话

    assembly n 议会，集会

  3. ong before the advent of the Roman Empire

    advent n 重要事件、人物、发明等的）出现

    the advent of new technology 新技术的出现

C8 T2

listening

Section 1

关于保险公司的话题

  1. 公司名称 Total Insurance

  2. I recently shipped my belongings from overseas back here to Australia

    ship … from … to … 海运

  3. take out insurance with your company 在你们公司投保

  4. make a claim / raise a claim 提出索赔

  5. shipping agent 船运代理商

  6. the point of origin 船运的出发地

  7. Mover 搬家公司（运送物品的机器（或人）；搬运家具的人）

    professional furniture movers 专业搬家公司

    an earth mover 运土机

  8. right-hand-corner

    in the top right-hand corner 在右上角

    in the bottom right-hand corner 在右下角

  9. cabinet

Section 2

  1. huge complex

    compex n 建筑群

Section 3

  1. seminar

    会议集锦
    seminar 研讨会 通常為中小型會議主題多元可為一次性討論，或為一系列講座
    conference 会议 通常為中型或大型會議多為正式會議主題多聚焦於商業或產業可為一次性或為期好幾天的演講
    convention xx大会、集会 大型會議與會者通常背景相似，如相同職業或政黨可以是正式會議，也可以較為輕鬆，例如粉絲見面會
    workshop 讨论会、工作坊 通常為小型會議或聚會與會者有實作機會與會者與講者互動性較高
    meeting （公司）会议 定義較廣的會議通常指辦公室內的會議人數較少，一對一也是會議
    forum 论坛、座谈 形式多元，如網路論壇主題多樣性
    summit 峰会 參加者多為各國首腦媒體曝光程度高討論議題多圍繞全球性問題

  2. specialize in identifying insects 从事昆虫鉴别

  3. over to you 接下来请你发言/接下里的时间交给你

  4. quarantine 检疫

    keep in quarantine

    quarantine service 检疫中心

  5. insect pest 害虫

    pet n 害虫；有害动物

    insect/plant/garden pests 害虫；作物╱花园害虫

    pest control 害虫防治

  6. import / export

  7. eradicate 消灭

    eradicate racism/illnesses/poverty

    eradicate illnesses
