Accumulation topic(6) 名词、动词、形容词、副词

本文最后更新于：2023年2月28日 下午

褒义

adj/adv
aesthetically 审美地 /esˈθetɪkli/
aesthetically appealing 具有美学吸引力的
stunning 极有魅力的 stunning view / stunningly beautiful

抽象

adj/adv
arbitrary 任意的；随机的
武断的		 /ˈɑːrbɪtreri/
Knowing as much relevant information as possible can help us avoid making arbitrary decisions. 武断的决定
Input can also have arbitrary arguments. 输入可含有任意参数。

描述

adj/adv
hydrating 保湿的 /ˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
It is hydrating and gentle. 这款产品保湿温和。
greasy 多油的；油腻的 /ˈɡriːsi/
greasy chips 油腻的炸薯条
long greasy hair 油性长发
binge-watchable 可以一口气看完的 short, binge-watchable videos
n
tail / tip 尾部 / 尖端 from the arow’s tail to the tip

动作

具象 verb
wedge 挤入；塞进 /wedʒ/
She couldn’t wedge another item into the suitcase. 她无法再往箱子里多塞一件东西了。
erode 侵蚀 Thr waves erode the rocks on the shore.
抽象 verb
obscur 掩盖；隐藏 There is hardly any theory which is more elementary than linear algebra, in spite of the fact that generations of professors and textbook writers have obscured its simplicity by preposterous calculations with matrices.
endanger 使危险；危及 If you work hard without rest, you will endanger your health.
endangered 濒临灭绝的
endangered species 濒危物种
jeapardize 威胁到；危及 He would never do anything to jeopardize his career. 他决不会做任何有损于他事业的事。
deteriorate 恶化 /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/
deteriorating weather conditions 不断恶化的天气状况
Relations between the two countries have deteriorated sharply in recent weeks.
Her health deteriorated rapidly.
aggravate 使（局势）变坏，使（病情）恶化 /ˈæɡrəveɪt/
aggravate the conflict/differences 加剧冲突/加深分歧
degrade 降解 degradable 可降解的
upgrade 升级；（设施、服务等的）改善 upgrade the town’s leisure facilities 改善镇里的休闲设施

搭配

verb

Soft drinks usaully contain carbon dioxide. 软饮料中通常含有二氧化碳。

adj/adv

The crust on the snow **was thick enough for us to walk **on it. 雪上冻结的冰很厚，足以让我们在上面行走。

be … enough for sb. to do sth.
